The Latency Crisis

Financial crime operates in real-time. Compliance operates on a delay.

For 20 years, I worked inside the world's largest financial institutions. I saw the same problem everywhere: The Batch Processing Bottleneck.

Banks rely on relational databases to catch criminals. But databases are static. They are designed to store records, not to simulate behavior. By the time a "Smurfing" ring or a "Layering" scheme is detected by a batch query, the money has already moved across three borders and turned into crypto.

The Zquas Solution

I founded Zquas to solve a physics problem in banking: Scale and Speed.

We do not use databases to detect risk. We use High-Performance Physics Simulation.

By treating money as a fluid and bank accounts as autonomous agents, we can simulate national-scale economies on a single server rack. We calculate risk the way a flight simulator calculates aerodynamics: constantly, instantly, and with physics-based precision.

Core Architecture

  • Sovereign Memory: A proprietary C++/CUDA architecture that bypasses standard driver bottlenecks, enabling zero-copy communication between Simulation (CPU) and AI (GPU).
  • Unified Topology: The only engine capable of simulating Fiat Banking (SWIFT/SEPA) and Digital Assets (UTXO/Account) in a single, unified graph.
  • Nuclear Scale: Validated capacity of 8,000,000+ active agents with < 20ms update loops.
